Tuna Poke
 Katsu Curry
 Dorayaki

Ingredients

  • Fresh Tuna
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Green Onions

Ingredients

  • Pork Cutlet
  • Curry Roux
  • Rice
  • Vegetables (Carrot, Potato)

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Sweet Red Bean Paste
  • Honey

Proedures

  1. Dice fresh tuna into small cubes.
  2. Mix with soy sauce, sesame oil, and chopped green onions.
  3. Chill in the fridge for 15 minutes and serve.

Proedures

  1. Coat pork cutlet with flour, egg, and breadcrumbs.
  2. Fry until golden brown and drain excess oil.
  3. Simmer curry roux with vegetables until tender.
  4. Serve curry over rice with sliced pork cutlet.

Proedures

  1. Mix flour, eggs, and honey to make batter.
  2. Cook small pancakes on a skillet.
  3. Sandwich sweet red bean paste between pancakes.
  4. Serve warm or at room temperature.
